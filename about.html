A Warm Reception

Book My Show is a online Application where you can book tickets sitting at your home. All you need is an working Internet Connection. Book movie tickets, events, and even host your own shows with tickets.

Meet Our Owner

Hello, my name is Sanjay Parmar and I am the inventor of BookMyShow, a Website inspired by my own personal ticket purchase experience. Spending the time in long cinema ticket lines were bad but times when tickets got sold out was worst. Often times I would return tired with bad mood and I knew it was time for a change! So I thought why there is a need to stand in those long lines and wait for your turn in this modern era of technology. And hence, we are here today, Book tickets for first show of your favourite movies sitting on your couch without any worry jus in couple of minutes.
